About

A career built on passion
and industry knowledge

Building on a foundation of ten years as a performing musician, Michael transitioned into the business side of music with a First Class degree from the Liverpool Institute for Performing Arts. During university, he volunteered at festivals, conferences and managed artists - using every opportunity to deepen his understanding of the industry and build lasting relationships with music professionals.

Most recently at Modern Sky Entertainment UK, Michael progressed from a part-time finance assistant into a multifaceted role covering day-to-day artist management, publishing administration, and campaign delivery - developing a holistic understanding of the music business and serving as a central point of contact between artists and their wider teams.

Highly organised, calm under pressure, and thriving in fast-paced environments, Michael is a reliable, detail-driven professional who keeps momentum moving while always prioritising the artist's best interests.
